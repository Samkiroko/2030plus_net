About 2030 plus

25 Years of Experience

2030 Plus is a consortium of like-minded professionals

2030 Plus is a consortium of like-minded professionals from the real estate and allied industries to develop turnkey real estate projects that would significantly and directly improve the socio-economic well-being of the people of Kenya and the greater East African population by pushing the boundaries of aesthetics and structure while leveraging the potential of innovation and technology in the Real Estate and Construction Sector.

We also aim to contribute significantly towards the affordable housing crisis in Kenya and are currently developing a model that works with the latest technologies to provide a fast modular construction option.

We work on an original concept every time, starting with an identified parcel of land that is available and creating a project that meets the approval of the land owner, developer and all the experts and team members.

Our Team

Mr. Sandeep Singh Rehal

Partner at Studio Infinity Architects

Sandeep is a founding Partner at Studio Infinity Architects and has worked as a Professional Architect for over 25 years. During this time he has been a project architect for various projects including Housing Developments, Hotels, Commercial Office Developments, and Shopping Malls.

Sandeep holds a Bachelor of Architecture (First Class Honours) from the University of Nairobi and is a Registered Architect of the Board of Registration of Architects and Quantity Surveyors of Kenya.

Mr. Amaan Pirani

sales and marketing consultant

Amaan has worked in the real estate industry, initially as a sales and marketing consultant for both local and international real estate agencies and more recently as a development management consultant for over 8 years. Currently, he is the founding partner at an on-demand garment and apparel manufacturing company as well as an advisor on the board of various technology startups creating solutions in the supply chain, financial services, and identity management sectors in Africa.

Amaan holds a Bachelor of Business Science in Economics from Monash University.

Mr. Gabriel Karanja Njenga

Partner at Karanja-Njenga Advocates

Mr. Karanja is the founding Partner at Karanja-Njenga Advocates, a fully-fledged Corporate Commercial Law firm, established in 1993. He holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from Sheffield University in Sheffield, UK. He attained a Diploma in Law from the Kenya School of Law in 1991 and has been an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya since 1993. He also holds a specialized Honours Degree Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Political Science from York University, Toronto, Canada, and is also a Certified Public Secretary.

Mr. Karanja is a Non-Executive Director and Member of the Board of The Unclaimed Financial Assets Authority (UFAA).

Mr. Alnoor Amlani

management consulting

Alnoor has over 20 years of professional management consulting & audit/accounting experience in East & Southern Africa gained with Deloitte, KPMG, Githongo & Co, and as an independent practitioner. He has experience in fundraising, business planning, financial analysis, forensic investigations, business valuations, feasibility studies, and due diligence work, as well as traditional audit and financial systems/management.

Alnoor is a qualified Chartered Accountant.
