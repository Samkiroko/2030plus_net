An effective Model

Joint Ventures (JV), Public-Private Partnerships (PPP) - Our Joint Venture (JV) models have been effective at bringing together owners of land with dormant potential and our development sponsors who carry out the real estate development process, producing assets that give healthy returns to both the developer and land owner and our financial syndication model is designed to unite medium-sized investors seeking to reap the benefits of real estate development.

As such, these investors have been able to generate significant returns on developments that are fully managed by our in-house team without having to get involved in the day-to-day operations of real estate development. On the other hand, Public-Private Partnerships are more important than ever in achieving planning and economic development goals, particularly in Africa. Our team has become intimate with how the private sector finances and executes real estate development because our core customers are private sector investors, developers, and builders.

We bring this knowledge to the table in our Public-Private Partnership implementation strategies, in that we develop public-sector development strategies that are appealing to private-sector partners. Also, we are skilled at communicating the upside potential as well as a go-between for public and private players.

2030PLUS is a team inspired to achieve as well as go beyond Kenya's National Vision 2030 Strategy.

Combining architecture, engineering, planning, cost control, budgetting and financial management, as well as raising capital and marketing, we aim to provide a complete solution for real estate projects in Kenya and the region.

CONCEPT STRUCTURING AND DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

A successful real estate development is determined at the planning and inception stages, where market dynamics are carefully studied to identify demand gaps which, if appropriately targeted, will result in high levels of end user uptake. To achieve this, we use a data-driven approach to identify gaps within each target market segment. As a result, our projects have high uptake rates in the segments we focus upon.

Development Feasibility

Managing real estate design, development and operations. Our feasibility work is prepared for landowners, investors, venture partners, and government agencies to assess the viability of new developments or re-purposed assets. Projects become feasible when all parties involved in a development or transaction see positive outcomes from their respective perspectives. Private owners, investors, regulatory players, tenants and local communities are all included throughout the process to generate the best possible outcome.

Construction & Project Management

We strive to achieve the best quality product in the best possible time at the most reasonable price. To accomplish this we manage a project from conceptualization to construction and handover. Collectively our team members have a significant number of years of combined experience. We have the expertise to determine which are the right elements to include to ensure a successful project. by managing and directing the project team and team members, establishing and observing project timelines while preserving dialogue with all the relevant stakeholders involved in the project regularly.

Asset & Facilities Management

A key factor in the value realization of any real estate development is the ongoing maintenance and management of the asset. To cater for this, we have established partnerships with local and international agents experienced, not only, in managing the investment but also looking after operational and maintenance issues.
